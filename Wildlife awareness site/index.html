Critically Endangered

Sumatran Tiger

With fewer than 400 individuals remaining in the wild, the
Sumatran tiger faces extinction due to poaching and
habitat loss.

Tropical Forests of Sumatra, Indonesia

Endangered

Mountain Gorilla

Mountain gorillas have faced severe threats from poaching,
habitat loss, disease, and human conflict.

Virunga Mountains, Central Africa

Vaquita Porpoise

The world's most rare marine mammal, with fewer than 10
